Some Recommendations

  • Use Google Chrome browser for a better user experience.
  • Keep your microphone and camera muted when possible to avoid extraneous noise and echoing. Headphones with built-in microphones give best performance.
  • Adjust video quality to match your available bandwidth
  • Meeting password is reset when all participants have left the meeting
